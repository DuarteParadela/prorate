Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my purchase?

To restore your lifetime purchase, open the App Settings, navigate to the premium section, and tap "Restore Purchase". Ensure you are logged in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

Where do exchange rates come from?

We source our exchange rate data from Exchange rate API, reliable third-party API that aggregate real-time financial market data to provide you with accurate conversions.

How do I change my base currency?

You can change your base currency by tapping on the flag icon or currency code on the main screen, then selecting your desired base currency from the comprehensive list.
